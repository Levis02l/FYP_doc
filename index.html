Project Description:

This project is designed to provide users with a comprehensive platform to track their fitness journey. It allows users to create and customize their own workout plans, track progress over time, and explore a wide range of exercises tailored to different fitness levels and goals. The platform ensures that users can select workouts based on muscle groups, available equipment, and personal preferences. Additionally, the website provides insightful data visualization tools that help users analyze their improvements, offering statistics on workout consistency and progression. By integrating a well-structured and user-friendly interface, the system encourages individuals to stay consistent with their fitness goals while providing a streamlined experience for managing workouts efficiently.
